Details

Time Limit: Each question has a specific time limit to answer.

One Answer: Only one answer can be submitted per question.

No Cheating: No use of external sources (e.g., internet, phones, or help from others).

Scoring: Points are awarded for correct answers, and sometimes penalties for incorrect ones.

No Spelling Mistakes: Answers must be spelled correctly unless otherwise stated.

Silence: Participants must remain silent while others are answering.

Tie-breaker: In case of a tie, a tie-breaker question may be used.
